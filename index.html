Reference Formulas (SAE J1526 / ISO 8855)

Tractive force F_total = m·a + m·g·sin(θ) + μ_r·m·g·cos(θ) + ½·ρ·Cd·A·v²
Wheel torque → Engine torque τ_engine = (F_total × r_wheel) / (GR × η)
Engine angular velocity ω = 2π·RPM / 60 (rad/s)
Engine power P = τ_engine × ω    [W] → [kW] ÷ 1000
Vehicle speed from RPM v = (2π·r·RPM) / (60·GR)  [m/s]
Traction limit (per axle) F_traction_max = μ_tyre × m·g × weight_bias
